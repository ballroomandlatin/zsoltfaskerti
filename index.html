    Zsolt Faskerti

    Professional Ballroom and Latin Instructor

    based in London, UK

    Zsolt has been dancing for over 20 years and continues to improve himself by participating in national and international competitions. He holds the Hungarian Latin show dance national champion title and has competed in over 28 countries around the world at the highest level. He has experience teaching students from their first step on the dance floor to competitive dancers. Zsolt is ready to share his knowledge and passion for dancing with greater emphasis in coordination, technique and styling.
